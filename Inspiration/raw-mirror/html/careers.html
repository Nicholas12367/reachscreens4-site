Who we are

Enter the world of Outdoor advertising and make your mark on the industry. As Canada’s leading provider of Out-of-Home Advertising, you’ll have the opportunity to shape urban landscapes and work with leaders in creative executions. With offices across the nation, check out our job listings available in your region.

Company culture

Valuing a diverse workforce’s unique perspectives and experiences, PATTISON Outdoor Advertising is dedicated to fostering an inclusive environment where all employees feel respected and empowered to contribute their ideas and talents. We actively support initiatives that promote diversity, equity and inclusion within the advertising industry and the wider community.

As leaders in Canadian advertising, we are determined to initiate change starting from within, lead by example and reflect the diversity of the communities within which we work and live, coast-to-coast.
