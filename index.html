Why It Works

Advertising Where Attention
Already Exists

Customers spend real time inside the restaurants, waiting rooms, and shops they visit. Your ad plays directly in front of them, again and again.

Captive Audience

Unlike billboards you drive past at highway speed, indoor screens reach people who are stationary, with their phones down or eyes up. They actually see your ad.

Repeated Exposure

Your ad plays on rotation throughout the day. The same customer sees it again and again across a single visit, building familiarity and trust that drives action.

Show up in restaurants, bars, retail shops, and offices across Lloydminster. Locals start recognizing you everywhere they go.
