About Reach Screens

Built in Lloydminster, For Lloydminster

Reach Screens was started with a simple idea: local businesses deserve an advertising option as modern as the national brands get, without the national brand price tag.

We install indoor digital screens inside local businesses across the city. Advertisers get high-frequency exposure in places where attention actually lives. Hosts get free screens, free advertising, and a better customer experience.

Everything is built, managed, and supported locally. No call centers. No corporate middlemen. Just a small team that cares about making local advertising work.
