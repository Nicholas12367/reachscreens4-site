How It Works

From Idea to On-Screen In Days

Two sides to every Reach Screens relationship. Pick the one that applies to you below.

1

Tell Us Your Idea

What are you promoting, who are you trying to reach, and when do you want it to run? We start with what you want to accomplish, then build the rollout around it.

2

We Recommend the Right Coverage

Based on your idea, timing, and audience, we'll suggest the package and locations that fit. No upselling — just what works for what you're trying to do.

3

We Design Your Ad (Free)

Our in-house design team creates a professional 15-second ad optimized for digital screens. You review and approve.

4

Ad Goes Live Across Lloydminster

Your ad plays again and again across the network throughout the day. We'll update it whenever your idea evolves.

1

Apply to Host a Screen

If your business has foot traffic and a good wall, you can host a Reach Screens display. Fill out a short form, we'll be in touch within 48 hours.

2

We Install the Screen

Professional installation at no cost to you. We handle hardware, mounting, and setup. You just give us a wall and an outlet.

3

Free Advertising for Your Business

As a host, your business gets rotation on our network across other locations, free advertising in exchange for hosting.
